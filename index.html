Synergistic Lead Flow Process

  1. 1
    Identification:

    ReHo Consulting identifies investment opportunities within its expanded client base (both gastronomy and general digital marketing).

  2. 2
    Assessment:

    Strategists perform an initial assessment of investment viability based on growth potential and market position.

  3. 3
    Referral:

    With client permission, ReHo Consulting makes a warm introduction to ReHo Holdings investment team.

  4. 4
    Nurturing & Transition:

    ReHo Holdings takes over, leveraging the initial trust built by ReHo Consulting to advance the investment conversation.

  5. 5
    Success Story:

    Successful investments become powerful case studies, demonstrating the ReHo Group's holistic value proposition.

Strategic Vision

By leveraging the expanded scope of ReHo Consulting and its synergistic relationship with ReHo Holdings, the group aims to create a comprehensive ecosystem that drives growth across all business units while providing exceptional value to clients and portfolio companies.

Key Success Factors

  • Maintaining strong brand alignment across all entities
  • Continuous refinement of lead sharing processes
  • Cross-training teams on identifying opportunities
  • Consistent quality across all services and subgroups
  • Data integration between consulting and investment arms

5-Year Goals

  • Establish ReHo as a recognized leader in both investment and marketing
  • Expand to 3+ new geographic markets
  • Launch the ReHo Incubator program
  • Develop proprietary technology for lead qualification
  • Create a diverse portfolio spanning multiple industries
